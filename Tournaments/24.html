    Format[edit]

    • Round Robin Bo5
    • Tiebreakers:
      • If two teams tie on match wins the Head to Head match determines seeding.
      • If three team tie on match wins the Head to Head match determines seeding. If the teams are still tied, game win percentage seeds the teams.

    Player Accolades[edit]

    • Golden Striker ($500 USD): Awarded to the clinical finisher who scores the most goals per game in League Play for their region.
    • Clutch Playmaker ($500 USD): Awarded to the cunning player who records the most assists per game in League Play for their region.
    • Savior of the Season ($500 USD): Awarded to the player who racks up the highest saves per game in League Play for their region.
    • Regular Season MVP ($1,000 USD): Awarded to the player who contributes the most to his team's success in League Play as determined by a panel of RLCS Analysts and administrators.
