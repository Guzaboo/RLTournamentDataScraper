    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage - April 7th - May 5th
      • Round Robin Bo5
        • Tiebreakers:
          • In the case of a tie on points game differential always seeds the teams.
      • Top two auto-qualify for the Finals
      • Top six qualify for the Regional Championship
      • Bottom two teams are relegated to the Promotion Playoffs
    • Regional Championship - May 12th
      • Bo7 Page Playoff System.
      • 5th and 6th start against each other in the Lower Bracket with 3rd and 4th in the Upper Bracket, playing a Double Elimination Bracket to determine the 2 other teams which qualify for the Finals.
      • The rest stands as a Single Elimination Bracket to determine seedings.
      • 3rd Place is determined via League Play tiebreakers.
      • Teams in the Regional Championship auto-qualify for RLCS Season 8.

    Player Accolades[edit]

    • Golden Striker ($1,000 USD): Awarded to the clinical finisher who scores the most goals per game in League Play for their region.
    • Clutch Playmaker ($1,000 USD): Awarded to the cunning player who records the most assists per game in League Play for their region.
    • Savior of the Season ($1,000 USD): Awarded to the player who racks up the highest saves per game in League Play for their region.
    • Regular Season MVP ($2,500 USD): Awarded to the player who contributes the most to his team's success in League Play as determined by a panel of RLCS Analysts and administrators.
