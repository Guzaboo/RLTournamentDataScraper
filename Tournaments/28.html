    RLCS Season 4 is the second RLCS season in 2017. The LAN Finals are taking place in Washington D.C. at the MGM National Harbor Theater. For the first time, season 4 introduces a second division, the RLRS.

    Broadcast Talent[edit]

    • Stage Host:
      •  Goldenboy (Alex Mendez)
    • Desk Host:
      •  Axeltoss (Alex Rodriguez)
    • Analysts:
      •  Gibbs (Randy Gibbons)
      •  Jamesbot (James Villar)
      •  yumi_cheeseman (David Lane)
      •  Karma (Jamie Bickford)
    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  FindableCarpet (Kevin Brown)
      •  Lawler (Adam Thornton)
      •  Quinn Lobdell (Michael Behrouzi)
      •  WavePunk (Caleb Simmons)
      •  Shogun (Callum Keir)

    Format[edit]

    • Double Elimination
      • All games Bo5, except: Winners', Losers', and Grand Finals. Those are Bo7.

    Trivia[edit]

    • This is the first RLCS LAN without FlipSid3 Tactics (specifically Markydooda and Kuxir97), remkoe, gReazymeister, Turtle or Sadjunior.
    • This is the first RLCS LAN without anyone from Scotland or Denmark playing.
    • The Mock-It eSports organisation has fielded a team at every RLCS LAN.
    • The NRG Esports roster has played at every RLCS LAN.
    • This is the RLCS LAN where there was the first ever twice-in-a-row winner, Turbopolsa.
