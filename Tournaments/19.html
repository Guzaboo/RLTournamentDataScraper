    About[edit]

    Broadcast talent[edit]

    • Host:
      •  Axeltoss (Alex Rodriguez)
    • Analyst:
      •  Gibbs (Randy Gibbons)
    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  FindableCarpet (Kevin Brown)
      •  WavePunk (Caleb Simmons)
      •  Quinn Lobdell (Michael Behrouzi)
      •  Subie_Smash (Travis Hale)

    Format[edit]

    • Double Elimination
      • All Matches are Bo7
    • Top two teams from the RLRS are matched with the bottom two teams from the RLCS
      • Top two teams will qualify for RLCS Season 5.
      • Bottom two teams will qualify for RLCS Season 5: RLRS

    Prize Pool[edit]

    Place $ USD Seed Team
    1st -  RLCS Season 5 Renegades
    2nd -  RLCS Season 5 Out of Style
    3rd -  RLCS Season 5: RLRS Allegiance
    4th -  RLCS Season 5: RLRS Fibeon eSports
