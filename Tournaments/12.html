    Broadcast talent[edit]

    English Broadcast[edit]

    French Broadcast[edit]

      •  Spleen (Houarnev Louaisel)

    Format[edit]

    • Round 1 - July 21st, 2017 [1]
      • Eight groups of up to four teams
      • Double elimination Bo3
      • Top two move on to round 2
      • Bottom two are eliminated
    • Round 2 - July 22nd, 2017 [1]
      • Four groups of four teams
      • Double elimination Bo5
      • Top two move on to the playoffs
      • Bottom two are eliminated
    • Playoffs - July 23rd, 2017 [1]
      • Single elimination bracket. First round is Bo5 while both semi-finals and finals are Bo7
