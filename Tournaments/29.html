    The 2017 ELEAGUE Cup: Rocket League is the first time ELEAGUE (see Counter-Strike wiki) hosts an event for Rocket League. American television channel TBS is also to present a three-part series documenting Rocket League most accomplished teams and players, which will air every week at 10 pm ET/PT on TBS, starting Friday December the 1st.

    Broadcast Talent[edit]

    English Broadcast[edit]

    • Hosts:
      •  Richard Lewis (Richard Lewis)
      •  Goldenboy (Alex Mendez)
    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  FindableCarpet (Kevin Brown)
      •  Lawler (Adam Thornton)
      •  Achieves (Michael Williams)
      •  WavePunk (Caleb Simmons)

    French Broadcast[edit]

    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  Boyan (Boyan Kaftandjiev)
      •  Spleen (Houarnev Louaisel)
      •  Bachibouzouk (Léo Bouchoux)

    Format[edit]

    • Group Stage
      • Round robin
      • All matches Bo5
        • Each win in Group Play awards the winning team an extra $1,000 in addition to their regular prize
      • Top two advance to playoffs
    • Playoffs
      • Single elimination - All matches Bo7
