    About[edit]

    Broadcast talent[edit]

    • Desk Host:
      •  Axeltoss (Alex Rodriguez)
    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  Achieves (Michael Williams)
      •  Quinn Lobdell (Michael Behrouzi)
      •  Jorby (Joey Ahrens)
      •  Fickle_Platypus (Grant Haynes)

    Format[edit]

    • 8 teams are invited.
    • 2 groups of 4 teams
      • Double elimination bracket Bo5
      • Top two move on
    • Top 4 decided by single elimination bracket Bo7

    Prize Pool[edit]

    $75,000 (USD) are spread among the teams as seen below: [1]

    Place $ USD Team
    1st $30,000 NRG Esports
    2nd $15,000 Gale Force eSports
    3rd-4th $7,500 Selfless Gaming
    Team Envy
    5th-6th $5,000 FlipSid3 Tactics
    Rogue
    7th-8th $2,500 G2 Esports
    The Leftovers
