    Overview[edit]

    Broadcast talent[edit]

    English Broadcast[edit]

    • Host:
      •  MRob (Marissa Roberto)
    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  Lawler (Adam Thornton)
      •  Liefx (Brody Moore)
      •  Subie_Smash (Travis Hale)
      •  Jorby (Joey Ahrens)

    French Broadcast[edit]

    • Commentator/Analysts:
      •  Boyan (Boyan Kaftandjiev)
      •  Spleen (Houarnev Louaisel)

    Format[edit]

    • Double Elimination Bo5, with the grand finals (and potential reset finals) Bo7.

    Prize pool[edit]

    $30,000 (USD) are spread among the teams as seen below:

    Place $ USD Team
    1st $12,000 Cloud9
    2nd $7,500 PSG Esports
    3rd $4,000 NRG Esports
    4th $2,500 Ghost Gaming
    5th-6th $1,500 Gale Force eSports
    Method
    7th-8th $500 G2 Esports
    Mirage Sport Electronique
