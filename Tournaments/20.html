    ThrowdownTV: Rocket League Challenge - Season 4 is the second time that Oceanic teams will be able to compete in the RLCS, the first time being RLCS Season 3 (also known as the ThrowdownTV Rocket League OCE Championship). This tournament was played at the Melbourne Convention and Exhibition Centre in Australia, as part of PAX AUS.

    Overview[edit]

    Broadcast Talent[edit]

    • Host/Casters:
      •  Hysterics (Jake Osypenko)
      •  CourierLord (Tom Sneddon)
      •  yumi_cheeseman (David Lane)

    Format[edit]

    • Double elimination
      • Bo5, with Bo7 in the winners, losers and grand finals.
    • Top two qualify for the RLCS Season 4 Finals and secure a spot in RLCS Season 5.

    Prize Pool[edit]

    $19,500 (USD) are spread among the teams as seen below:

    Place $ USD Seed Team
    1st $8,250 RLCS Season 4 Finals Chiefs Esports Club
    2nd $5,000 RLCS Season 4 Finals Pale Horse eSports
    3rd $3,750 - JAM Gaming
    4th $2,500 - Scylla Esports
